ABOUT ALU


OUR MISSION

We are an institution aimed at crafting the leaders of tommorow. The younger generation are the ones that will transform Africa in ways we cannot imagine. By integrating students learning with the real world, empowering students to take ownership of their own learning, equipping each student to think entrepreneurially, and employing the most engaging and inspiring teaching methods, ALU is pioneering a new take on higher education and leadership development.

ABOUT ALU

ALU aims to develop 3 million ethical and entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and the world by 2035. It uses a personalized, student-driven, project-based, and mission-oriented approach to create agile, lifelong learners who can adapt to a changing world
